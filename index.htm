About Me

Hi, I’m Chloe Samaha. After landing my dream job at a women-led PE firm, I quickly realized how workplace dynamics can limit potential. That led me to create BOND, a platform designed to help managers lead better and unlock their teams' full potential. If this resonates with you, feel free to reach out—I’d love to connect!
